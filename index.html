"Big brand guitars at small prices!"

Welcome to The Guitar Co. Established in 2024, we sell an array of electric and acoustic guitar brands such as Gibson, Martin, Taylor and many more!

Did you know, we have supplied guitars for bands such as U2, Aslan and the Wolfe Tones to name a few?
You can trust our quality and prices. We even offer a 30 day money-back guarantee just for extra piece of mind!

Take a look at our guitars. We believe that each guitar we stock should be played before taking it home, therefore we take reservations through our online contact form

The Guitar Co. was established in 2024 by a group of Musicians who wanted more for the music "scene" in Dublin's Fair City.
We believe that anyone can learn to play guitar and therefore we ensure through our fair pricing, that our guitars are accessible to anyone, regardless of their experience.
You will also see on our events page that we host a range of events around Dublin every month for players of all levels.
